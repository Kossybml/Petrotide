Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our bunkering, bulk fuel supply and logistics services.

Do you supply bulk fuel in Lagos and across Nigeria?

Yes. Petrotide Energies Limited supplies bulk PMS, AGO, DPK, ATK, LPG and LPFO to clients within Lagos and across other key locations in Nigeria. We serve marine operators, industrial facilities, commercial fleets and distributors through structured delivery schedules and reliable logistics.

What types of petroleum products do you offer?

Our product slate includes PMS, AGO, DPK, ATK, LPG, LPFO, polypropylene, base oil and fertilizers. These products support marine operations, power generation, manufacturing, transportation and agricultural activities across West Africa.

Do you provide bunkering services for offshore vessels in Nigeria?

Yes. We provide bunkering services for offshore supply vessels, tankers and other marine assets operating in Nigerian waters. Our team focuses on safe transfer procedures, product quality, accurate metering and timely delivery to minimize downtime and operational risk.
